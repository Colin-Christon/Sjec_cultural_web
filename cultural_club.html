CULTURAL CLUB

To enrich the life of students by promoting art, literature and culture.

To promote and arrange extracurricular activities and to bring out the talents of students.

  • The Cultural Committee shall be responsible for all intra college cultural competitions/events.
  • To conduct competitions for literary events like debates, elocutions, and so on within the college.
  • To encourage students to attend literary events outside the college.
  • To organize cultural events.
  • To communicate about various festivals and events to be celebrated in the college and give wide publicity.
  • Arranging events/programs for students in coordination with 'Cultural Clubs'.
